Potable Water

In Moula Ali, residents have access to Manjeera water every alternate day for 2 hours. However, in some areas like Kasturi Nagar, near Moula Ali junction, the limited provision of Manjeera water poses a significant issue. In this locality, the municipality offers water for a mere 3-hour duration, but only once every 4-5 days, which creates water scarcity problems for the residents.
