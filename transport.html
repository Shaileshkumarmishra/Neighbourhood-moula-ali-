Transport

Regarding road conditions, there are challenges in safely crossing roads, as exemplified by the situation at the ECIL Junction. In this location, there is a lack of proper facilities for pedestrians to safely cross the road, which has contributed to accidents.

Accidents are a recurring issue at both the ECIL junction and Moula Ali junction, particularly in areas near educational institutions such as Kamala Nagar. These accidents often take place during the evening peak hours, primarily attributed to inadequate road conditions in the vicinity.
