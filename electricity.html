Electricity

Subhash D serves as the council for the Moula Ali division, which comprises 13 sections. Within this division, there are 10 sub-stations, each with an average voltage of 20-25k kilovolts per sub-unit.

The electricity substation near Banda Cheruvu has a capacity of 33,000 kilowatts (kW) and serves 15 colonies within a 25,000-meters.

The power supply experiences interruptions twice a year due to tree cutting.

According to an officer at the substation, it is evident that there is a pressing need to expand the number of substations in the Moula Ali area. This expansion is essential to guarantee a more reliable and stable power supply, ultimately mitigating power interruptions and meeting the increasing demand from the local population.
