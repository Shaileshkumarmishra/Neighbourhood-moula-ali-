Subhash D serves as the council for the Moula Ali division, which comprises 13 sections. Within this division, there are 10 sub-stations, each with an average voltage of 20-25k kilovolts per sub-unit.

There are two drainage channels in Moula Ali. One follows the Ecil route, while the other follows the Banda Cheruvu path. Both of these drainage channels eventually merge into the Moosi River.

In Moula Ali, residents have access to Manjeera water every alternate day for 2 hours. However, in some areas like Kasturi Nagar, near Moula Ali junction, the limited provision of Manjeera water poses a significant issue

For cooking, the majority of people use gas cylinders, while a few opt for electric stoves. Those using gas cylinders often find it to be a more expensive cooking option.
